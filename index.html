IEEE Chandigarh University Student Branch(IEEE CUSB), a vibrant student branch with 620+ members stands first in IEEE India Council and never misses  any  chance to give students best  opportunities. IEEE CUSB believes  in  continuous  learning process  that  helps  students  to implement their learning in the form of Research  Papers,  Patents  and  Projects.


IEEE CUSB organise multiple events to enhance students technical knowledge. One such event is Tech-A-Month, a month long flagship event that consists of series of events in different domains. We organise Expert Talks, Workshops and Competitions in domains like Web Development, Machine Learning, Blockchain, Cyber Security, Research, Patents, Career Planning, Placement Strategies and many more.

 

During the Tech-A-Month series , we conduct both technical and non technical events that help students to not only brush up their skills but also explore different domains too . Last year , we were able to successfully host and organise Tech-A-Month with different events like "Open-source, Git and GitHub", "GATE Preparation for CSE", "Art of A.I.", "Aatm Nirbhar Bharat", "How to get your dream placement", "Journey of Innovation and engineering", "E-mobility in India" and many more. We will be accompanied by dignitaries like Mr. Love Babbar, Mr. Tanmay Arora, Ms. Bishakha Jain, Abdul Bari Mohh., Ms. Shristi Singh, and Mr. Ramneek Kalra, and many others. We are also organizing a Hackathon this time in Tech-a-Month 3.0 that will help students to work on their innovative ideas and implement them.


In 2022 , we are planning to organise Tech-A-Month 3.0 from 15 July 2022 to 15 August 2022 and we wish for your support and collaboration.
We request you for sponsorship in Tech-A-Month 3.0 2022.


Looking forward to a positive confirmation , an honour for us indeed.
